FIGURE SKATING

Figure skating is a sport where skaters perform routines on ice, combining jumps, spins, and footwork. It's known for its elegance, as skaters move to music while showing off their skills. There are different types of figure skating:

1. Singles: One skater performs alone, doing jumps, spins, and other moves.

2. Pairs: Two skaters (a man and a woman) perform together, often lifting each other and doing synchronized moves.

3. Ice Dance: This is more focused on dancing and rhythm, with fewer jumps.

It's an Olympic sport, and skaters are judged on their technique, creativity, and how well they perform their routine.
