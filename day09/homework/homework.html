lets make Khinkali

  1. for dough:
    1. flour
    2. salt
    3. warm water
    4. mix
    5. rest if for 30 minutes
  2. for filling:
    1. meat
    2. onion
    3. garlic
    4. seasonings
    5. add some water
    6. mix
  3. roll the dough:
    1. divide into two parts
    2. cut it into small pieces
    3. roll each piece into a thin circle
  4. fill the Khinkali:
    1. fill the each dough piece with meat
    2. Lift the edges of the dough and pleat them together at the top
  5. cook the Khinkali:
    1. boil large pot of salted water
    2. put Khinkali gently in water
    3. cook for 10-12 minutes
    4. remove Khinkali from there
    5. pick and eat it (WITHOUT FORK AND KNIFE), bon appetit
