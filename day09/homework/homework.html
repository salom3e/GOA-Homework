    lets make Khinkali

    for dough:

  1. flour
  2. salt
  3. warm water
  4. mix
  5. rest if for 30 minutes

    6. for filling:

  6. meat
  7. onion
  8. garlic
  9. seasonings
  10. add some water
  11. mix

    12. roll the dough:

  12. divide into two parts
  13. cut it into small pieces
  14. roll each piece into a thin circle

    15. fill the Khinkali:

  15. fill the each dough piece with meat
  16. Lift the edges of the dough and pleat them together at the top

    17. cook the Khinkali:

  17. boil large pot of salted water
  18. put Khinkali gently in water
  19. cook for 10-12 minutes
  20. remove Khinkali from there
  21. pick and eat it (WITHOUT FORK AND KNIFE), bon appetit <3
