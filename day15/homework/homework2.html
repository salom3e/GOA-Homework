Hello, this one is about my favorite movies. As always, there's so many, but i can choose my top top ones.

Saw franchise



This is the classic one, it was 10 movies and eleventh one is coming out in 2025. The Saw franchise is about twisted games designed by the Jigsaw Killer (John Kramer) to test people's will to live through brutal, often deadly traps. It explores themes of morality, survival, and justice. I don't know what to say, just watch it and you will want to watch others.


Interstellar



Interstellar is about astronauts traveling through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity, exploring themes of love, time, survival, and the relationship between science and emotion. It is so well made, an amazing. I wish i could watch it for the fisrt time again, as Saw.


Dead Poets Society



Dead Poets Society is about an inspiring teacher who encourages students at a conservative boys' school to embrace poetry, think freely, and seize the day (carpe diem), challenging societal norms. I go to Manana Begiashvili's literary workshop, where she teaches us the same thing: how to think freely, be psychologically strong and successful. You can search for it on social networks. The Shining


The Shining is about a writer, Jack Torrance, who becomes the winter caretaker of the haunted Overlook Hotel, where he descends into madness, threatening his family. It explores isolation, supernatural forces, and psychological horror. I love psychological horrors.
