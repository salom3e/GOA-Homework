Legendary Artists

These are the artists who have shaped the sound of rock and metal:

James Hetfield (Metallica)

The frontman of Metallica, Hetfield's iconic voice and riffs have become legendary in rock history.

Jonathan Davis (Korn)

The leader of Korn, Davis brought a new level of emotion and intensity to metal with his unique style.

Freddie Mercury (Queen)

Mercury's vocal range and stage presence made Queen one of the most iconic rock bands ever.
